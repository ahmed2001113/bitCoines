IPO Policy

The maximum offering limit will be five tokens per individual to ensure the widespread adoption of the currency and increase the number of members in our community.
The initial price will be $100 per token.

The currency will be listed on all global platforms after 90% of the total supply has been subscribed to, ensuring there are no large quantities available for liquidation, which will continuously drive up the price towards the target value.
Regarding the developers' share, which amounts to 10%, it will be gradually released after two years of trading over a span of five years, at a rate of 2% per year.
This policy will maintain the decentralization principle, which is the cornerstone of our project, preventing any entity from hoarding and manipulating the price.
We kindly ask everyone to adhere to the subscription limit of five tokens per purchaser to ensure benefits for all.
